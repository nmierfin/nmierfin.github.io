The Others

Textbook Collecting

I believe that the best way to learn something is to get it from the source. That being said I think that many of today's textbooks often water down theories since the author also learned it from a second hand source. Therefore, the best way to learn the material is from the one who came up with it. This could be by learning semiconductor physics from William Bradford Shockley or information theory from Claude Shannon. The textbooks written by such authors have long been gone out of print therefore I enjoy collecting them as a means of preserving the knowledge.
